Our Vision.

Our vision is to become drivers of change by promoting youths with knowledge and encouraging them to use their maximum potential as citizens and as economic subjects and ensuring youth transition well into adulthood with the skills, capacities and sustainable livelihoods to overcome their challenges.

Our Missions.

Our mission is to join up young innovative and talented youths through Youth Empowerment, Environmental conservation, talent and sports, sanitation and hygiene in training and equipping them with enough knowledge.

Our Objective

To promote transformative youth empowerment with focus on employability and life skills, talent and entrepreneurship development. To promote youth participation in community service, peace building and leadership

To link up youths inorder to interact in their area of specialisation to promote good connection in their market environment.

To open ways for vulnerable youths inorder to promote the growth of our economy.

To promote and enjoy our African culture.

To encourage partnership with other actors or stakeholders.

To improve the livelihood and wellbeing of the youths.

To inculcate creativity and innovation among the youths.
